About Me

Hi! I'm Moiz. I am currently a Computer Science student at the University of Toronto, which means most of my time is spent working on proofs or solving problems that require me to traverse through trees of some sort.

Outside of school, I like to spend my time learning about various technologies, whether it be through working on side projects, or attending hackathons. On weekends, I am a private school teacher. Some of my other interests include, entrepreneruship and fitness.

Feel free to connect with me on LinkedIn, check out my projects on github, or read some of my articles on medium!
